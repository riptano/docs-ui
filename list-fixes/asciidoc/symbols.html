Em dash

--

&#8212;

 — 

Only replaced if between two word characters, between a word character and a line boundary, or flanked by spaces.

When flanked by space characters (e.g., a -- b), the normal spaces are replaced by thin spaces (&#8201;). Otherwise, the em dash is followed by a zero-width space (&#8203;) to provide a break opportunity.
